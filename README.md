# New Wheels SQL Business Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the quarterly business performance of New Wheels, a vehicle resale company, using SQL and business analytics techniques. The objective was to identify the factors contributing to declining sales, reduced customer satisfaction, and operational inefficiencies.

The analysis focuses on customer behavior, revenue trends, shipping performance, and customer feedback to support executive decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

New Wheels experienced declining sales and customer satisfaction over four consecutive quarters. Management required a business performance report containing key performance indicators and actionable insights to improve overall business performance.

## Key Performance Indicators

* Total Revenue: $82.6 Million
* Total Orders: 1,000
* Total Customers: 994
* Average Customer Rating: 3.07
* Average Shipping Time: 105 Days

---

## Analysis Performed

* Customer satisfaction analysis
* Revenue trend analysis
* Quarterly sales analysis
* Shipping performance evaluation
* Customer rating analysis
* State-level performance analysis
* Vehicle brand performance analysis

---

## Key Findings

* Customer satisfaction declined significantly across quarters.
* Revenue decreased over time.
* Shipping times increased and negatively affected customer experience.
* Order volume declined in later quarters.
* Certain vehicle brands consistently performed better than others.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Improve shipping and delivery efficiency.
* Reduce delivery lead times.
* Enhance customer service processes.
* Monitor customer feedback regularly.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products and regions.
* Implement KPI reporting for continuous performance monitoring.
